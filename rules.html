Ladder Rules
Players may challange either of the two players ahead of them.
If the challanger wins the match, the winner takes the losers position, and everyone below is moved down one rank.
Challange results should be notified to Leigh or Aodhagán.
When a player is challanged they have max 1 week to arrange to play the match, if they dont within the week they forfeit the match.
If a match is arranged, and one player fails to tur up, he/she forfeits the match.
If a player refuses a valid challange, he/she forfeits the match.
Any new player who wants to join will have to challange one of the bottom two players. After that match has been completed they will be added to the list in their resulting place.
If someone wishes to leave the ladder they will be removed from the list and everyone below will be bumped up a place.
